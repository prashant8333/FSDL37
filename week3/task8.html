About Father Agnel College

Father Agnel College of Engineering and Technology, established in 1996, is a prominent educational institution located in Vashi, Navi Mumbai. The college is dedicated to providing quality education in various fields of engineering, technology, and management, guided by the principles of Father Agnel.
